Frequently Asked Questions
Can I cancel anytime? ›
Yes. Cancel any time from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees.
Is my team's data private? ›
Absolutely. All cognitive data is anonymized and aggregated. No individual is ever identified by name in team dashboards. We never sell your data. GDPR-compliant by design.
What payment methods do you accept? ›
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and more. Payments are processed securely by Stripe. We never store your card details.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ›
Yes. Upgrade at any time and the price difference is prorated. Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle.
Do you offer student or nonprofit discounts? ›
Yes! We offer 50% off for verified students and nonprofits. Email us at hello@neuroweather.ai with your student ID or nonprofit status.
How does the AI detect my cognitive state without wearables? ›
NeuroWeather uses circadian rhythm science and behavioral pattern analysis — time of day, day of week, calendar density, and interaction cadence — to model cognitive readiness. No wearables or biometric devices are needed.
